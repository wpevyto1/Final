How it all Started...

In December of 2020, my son Mason turned six months old. With the COVID pandemic spiking, I was cooped up in the house and I figured it was a perfect opportunity to GO ALL OUT and celebrate his half birthday! I mean my husband and I survived 6 months of being brand new parents... during a pandemic. If that’s not worth celebrating, I don't know what is!

I went ALL OUT! I made a birthday banner, a crown, paper fan decorations, balloon bundles, a "half" birthday cake, cake pops and crocheted him a baby Sloth as a half-birthday present. All for my husband and I to enjoy together... just us... and the baby... and the dog.

Did I stay up WAY too late (the night before) making all of these things? Absolutely! Do I regret any of it? NO... except maybe the cake pops... those are a nightmare to make!

So all of this to say that, this is what sparked the actual opening of my business. I posted pictures of my creations on social media and was flooded with messages asking me to make them a special creation for their own kids. I then decided to create other animals and see what would happen. I once again was messaged by more and more people to create for them.

If you've made it to the end of this rambling novel... CONGRATS, you win my deepest appreciation!

Love Always,

Kaitlyn Joy
